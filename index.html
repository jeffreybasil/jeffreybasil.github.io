Is Jeffery Thirsty?

Jeffrey Basil - Absolute Unit. Looking out over the Hudson, soaking in the sun, a bushy green plant dominates his sill. Known as the baddest basil around, Jeffrey is secretly a softie. Skip watering him for one day and he becomes so dramaticly wilted, you almost feel bad for the poor guy. Dramatics aside Jeffrey has a great yield and has even fathered a youth. New York born and raised.
